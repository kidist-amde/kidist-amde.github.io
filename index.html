Ph.D. Research Interest Area

My research interest centers around creating explainable deep learning models that can mitigate biases towards historically marginalized groups in generative models. Specifically, my goal is to investigate responsible practices for text and image generation and examine the biases inherent in generative models such as Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and Diffusion probabilistic models (DPMs). Furthermore, I am interested in exploring in investigating how modern Language Models (LLMs) tend to give more importance to increasing the number of parameters and training data without adequate transparency or careful discretion of the data used. Ultimately, my main objective is to identify responsible practices for working with generative AI that addresses bias-related issues and promote transparency in the field.
